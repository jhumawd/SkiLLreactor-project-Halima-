About Us

Launched by former Amazon and Google engineers,
SkillReactor is a global tech company which aims to
close the software engineering skills gap through its
virtual work experience platform. The SaaS platform
delivers in-depth work experience through a simulated
work environment and industry standard projects for
engineers, allowing them to refine their skills.
Providing virtual work experience, SkillReactor enables
new and existing software engineers to gain relevant
experience and develop their industry understanding
on fully-fledged projects in a programming language
of their choosing. It allows users to operate, code,
and problem solve in an industry simulated environment
and helps them learn best practices by providing feedback
through its automated code review feature.
